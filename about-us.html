WHO WE ARE

Sahel Politica is an expert in political and security risk and crisis management in West Africa. With over 17 years' experience, we help organizations navigate through the geopolitical complexities of the Sahel while protecting their people, assets and reputation.

Our services are designed to identify, anticipate and mitigate the risks that threaten field operations. We offer tailor-made solutions to ensure the safety, stability and resilience of projects in challenging environments.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to help humanitarian organizations and other development players anticipate and mitigate political and security risks in the Sahel. Thanks to our expertise, we enable our customers to focus on their social impact while securing their operations and teams in an unstable environment.

We work alongside our partners to develop effective resilience strategies, combining risk analysis, crisis management and operational support. Our training courses, audits and prevention plans are specially designed to enable each organization to react quickly and effectively to the geopolitical challenges of the region.

OUR VISION

We aspire to a future where organizations working for social change in Africa can operate without fear of political and security risks. We believe that by being well prepared, every player can ensure the sustainability of their projects, strengthen their reputation, and build trusting partnerships with local communities and authorities.

Our vision is to create a framework where organizations can focus fully on their humanitarian or development missions, without being paralyzed by uncertainty. By fostering an environment of collaboration with local stakeholders, we aim to transform risk into opportunity, enabling organizations to thrive even in the most volatile contexts.

Dr. Awalou Ouedraogo
Associated Consultant

African governance, peacebuilding & International relations expert

Dr. Awalou Ouedraogo

Awalou Ouedraogo is a professor of international law and Director of the Human Rights and Equity Department at York University, Toronto. He holds a PhD from the Graduate Institute of International and Development Studies in Geneva and has published extensively on international law. His latest book is Due Diligence in International Law (2021). His research covers international law, human rights, migration, and state-building in Africa. A Knight of the National Order of Burkina Faso since December 2021, he is active in several scholarly associations and has participated in numerous international conferences.

Dr. Abdourahmane Idrissa
Associated Consultant

African politics and Ideologies specialist

Dr. Abdourahmane Idrissa

Abdourahmane Idrissa is a political scientist and historian based at Leiden University’s Africa Studies Centre and LASDEL in Niamey. He has extensively researched the Sahel, authoring The Politics of Islam in the Sahel and numerous articles in English and French. His focus includes the “regalian” state, democratic governance, and political Islam. He is currently writing a monograph on the Sahel during the 16th century using the Songhay Empire as a case study, and his recent fieldwork addresses the security crisis in Burkina Faso, Mali, and Niger.
