Questions ?

What type of training programs do you offer in geopolitical risk analysis?
We offer a range of programs including workshops, seminars, and online courses focusing on risk identification, analysis methods, scenario planning, and crisis management in the Sahel and the wider West-African region.
Who can benefit from your geopolitical risk management training?
Our training programs are designed for business executives, government officials, security professionals, analysts, and anyone involved in decision-making in politically volatile environments.
Do you offer customized training programs for specific industries?
Yes, we customize our training modules based on the specific geopolitical risks facing your industry, such as energy, finance, manufacturing, or telecommunications.
Can your training solutions help our team prepare for geopolitical crises?
Absolutely. Our training includes practical exercises and simulations that prepare teams to respond effectively to geopolitical crises, ensuring quick decision-making and crisis mitigation.
What kind of analytical tools are covered in your training courses?
We cover a range of analytical tools including risk matrices, forecasting models, scenario planning frameworks, and country risk ratings that help in assessing and managing geopolitical risks.
Are your training programs available online?
Yes, we offer both in-person and online training advs. Our online training programs are flexible and can be completed at your own pace, making it easy for teams in different locations to participate.
How long do your training courses typically last?
The length of our training courses varies based on the depth of content, ranging from half-day workshops to multi-day immersive programs depending on the level of detail and customization required.
Do participants receive certifications upon completing your training?
Yes, participants receive a certification of completion that verifies their training in geopolitical risk analysis and management. This can be valuable for professional development and demonstrating expertise.
How do you ensure that your training is up to date with current geopolitical risks?
Our training programs are continuously updated to reflect the latest geopolitical developments, trends, and case studies, ensuring participants receive the most current and relevant insights.
Can you conduct in-house training for our organization?
Yes, we offer in-house training tailored to your organization’s specific needs, allowing your team to receive focused, relevant training in the context of your own operational environment and risks in West-Africa.
