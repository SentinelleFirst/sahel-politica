SAHEL RESILIENCE AUDIT

Review your risk policy and local sites exposure.

This is the first step to navigate the complex and volatile environment of West African geopolitics and avoid political and security incidents. We work with you to create your risk portfolio, identify, and monitor your risk exposure. You will discover the risks to which your operations are exposed to. We will assess your operations, personnel, and assets exposure to local risk.

What will you gain at the end of the assessment?

  • Clarity on the nature of the current crisis you are facing
  • Clarity about your project’s exposure to local risks.
  • Clarity about onsite personnel security
  • Clarity about local risk trends and stakeholders.
  • Initial, actionable recommendations for your path out of the crisis.
  • Recommendations for crisis preparedness mechanisms improvement
  • Understanding on how to address weaknesses so that your organization become resilient in the face of crisis.

To accomplish this audit, we work with you to:

  • Review your sites security and personnel safety plans to identify risks before they arise (field visit).
  • Review your risk policies, regulations, procedures, and related documents.
  • Interview key HQ and 2 local program directors and staff members to capture and mapping of risk plan and procedures pain points.

Once the gap analysis is complete, we can move onto :

Step 3:

Leverage risks and seize opportunities.

SAHEL RISKS PREVENTION BLUEPRINT

Monitor, Understand and Anticipate Your Risks.

In this stage, we will work with you to create frameworks, plans and procedures to monitor, understand and forecasting the risks to your program’s portfolio.

At end of the review and redesign process, you will gain the following benefits :

  • Clarity on the step-by-step process to detect, monitor, and anticipate risks to your local projects.
  • Effective forecasting frameworks and future scenarios to support your risk planning process.
  • Effective risk monitoring frameworks and implementation plans to support your strategy formulation at the country/regional level.
  • Improvement of risk analysis skills for in-house analysts, risk managers, and executives.
  • Resolution of gaps in risk analysis and mitigation within the organization.
  • Formulation of strategy and risk mitigation plans at the country/regional level within the organization.

To reach these results, we work with you to:

  • Review the risk monitoring function, strategy, regulations, and procedures at HQ and local program levels to identify pain points in the monitoring/anticipation process.
  • Design and implement early warning systems to support risk monitoring plans.
  • Design and implement crisis scenarios to support strategy formulation at the country/regional level within the organization.
  • Educate and train in-house analysts or risk managers on political risk analysis so they are equipped with the frameworks, methodologies, tools, and contextual local knowledge to mitigate risks and handle crises.
  • Coach executives so they have a clear understanding of what it takes to successfully work with stakeholders.
  • Embed a political risk expert vetted by us in your team to build capacity and/or fill capability gaps.

Once your frameworks, plans and procedures for monitoring, understanding, and forecasting the risks to your program’s portfolio are set, we move onto :

SAHEL CRISIS RESPONSE PLAN

Plan For, Respond and Recover from Crisis.

The goal at this stage is to optimize your crisis response efficiency so that you will get:

What are the benefits that you will get?

  • Monitoring and assessment plans to understand key risks, anticipate stakeholder reactions, identify, and prioritize potential threats.
  • Incident management plans to prepare for different crisis scenarios and plan for an accelerated exit.
  • Security plans and scenarios to protect your people, including those you leave behind, as well as your assets, and move them out of the country safely, while also securing any facilities and preventing damage.
  • Communication strategy to plan stakeholder management and limit reputational risks.
  • Two briefings for the executive board on current political, strategic, and security issues.
  • One scenario planning workshop on how future developments may impact the client's exposure to risk and operational disruption.

To reach these results, we work with you to:

  • Test your framework for managing political risk within your organization, considering local humanitarian, social, and political changes.
  • Develop new tools, templates, processes, and governance frameworks for managing political risk within your organization, based on what you want your aid programs to achieve.
  • Benchmark the resilience of your risk management framework against the best practices of other organizations in the humanitarian landscape.
  • Train your in-house analysts, risk managers, and executives in incident/emergency response.
  • Embed our experts in your organization to support your crisis response team.

After 6 months of work

Once your frameworks, plans and procedures to respond to and recover from crisis are optimized, we can move onto:

Step 1:

Leverage risks and seize opportunities.

SAHEL OPPORTUNITY BOOSTER

Seize Opportunities for Market Entry/Expansion/Exit.

You have a clear vision of what you want the organization to be. To achieve your goals, we conduct a comprehensive analysis and provide you with a customized risk management strategy.

Our services include :

  • Country Due Diligence/Risk Assessments: We provide a detailed view of the political, security, operational, and social risks specific to your project, enabling you to make an informed decision on whether to proceed with a risk mitigation plan or not.
  • Feasibility Study Reports: We provide you with a detailed report on the feasibility of your project, providing information to inform your go/no-go decision and risk mitigation plan.
  • Stakeholder Mapping: We identify key decision-makers and actors who are impacted by your program, allowing you to develop a stakeholder engagement and government relations strategy.
  • Partner Due Diligence: We help you benchmark potential local partners against your own compliance and integrity criteria.
  • Location Benchmarking: We provide you with location benchmarking based on granular threat exposure assessments, enabling you to decide on the best project/office location.

To reach these results, we work with you to:

  • Assess your organization's exposure, risk tolerance, and interests regarding Entry/Expansion/Exit issues.
  • Assess the motivations driving your Entry/Expansion/Exit decisions.
  • Identify roadblocks and enabling factors for a successful Entry/Expansion/Exit operation in the area/country/aid cluster.
  • Provide 3 briefings for the executive board on current political, strategic, and security issues.
  • Conduct 2 scenario planning workshops on how future developments may impact your Entry/Expansion/Exit decisions.
