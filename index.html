About us

We come with many years of experience in Intelligence analysis of African political and security affairs.

Our mission

Sahel Politica helps clients navigate the complex and volatile environment of West African geopolitics and avoid political and security incidents in the Sahel region so that they can make an impact for the greater good.

With 17+ years of experience in Intelligence analysis of African political and security affairs, our proven Sahel risk and crisis preparedness™ framework enable our clients to invest or deliver aid response in volatile context while reducing security incidents in areas such as personnel and assets security or reputational damage.

Our vision

  • Are you a leader in supporting positive social change in Africa ?
  • Are you struggling to detect, assess and mitigate risks
  • Are you challenged to sustain the normal operations of your business or local aid programs?
  • Do you strive to protect and promote confidence in your organization’s reputation with local stakeholders?
  • Does uncertainty cause you loss of money, time, and trust?
It doesn’t have to be. You can operate from a place of resilience to risks:
  • Where time and resources are dedicated to growth and social impact, not worry and stress.
  • Donors and investors enthusiastically invest greater resources in your project.
  • Your organization is recognized for the value and growth it generates in fragile contexts.
  • Local audiences and stakeholders support you, not work against you.
  • Your organization is trusted by local authorities as a cooperation or business partner.
